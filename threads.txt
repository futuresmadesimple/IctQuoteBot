Look past the image lifestyle.
Don’t be a slave to image.
Many live above their income to put on a facade.
Write down what your life costs each month; make a budget.
Peace of mind and financial solitude are better than image.
---
Approach your life as a business and run your life as a business.
Cast forth a vision: where are you going, what’s your objective, what’s your goal?
What’s your routine—daily and weekly—and how do you handle drawdown?
Income is your only working resource unless you add income streams.
Lower your expenses and start preparing to think like a business owner.
---
Change the state of mind from impossible to I’m possible.
If you don’t believe it’s possible for you, nothing you learn will help you.
Guard your mind.
Pressure from the real world makes you abandon rules and over-leverage.
The chart reflects your life pressures unless you control them.
---
Demo is not live—it's a different world.
Can you build wealth in trading? Absolutely—but nobody starts there.
Only consider trading for a living when you’re properly funded—about $100,000 or more.
Use funded accounts to seed your own retail account.
Protect your access to the funded account—follow the rules.
---
Learn risk management well—and make that your initial, very first thing to master.
One trade should never be able to wipe out the account.
Taking partials reduces risk while paying you.
You want the holy grail? This is it: risk control.
Stop worrying about the money—scared money makes no money.
